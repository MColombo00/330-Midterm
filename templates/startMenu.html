Hello! Welcome to my Flask calculator program.

To start, enter the listed routes below into the URL:

  • For Addition you insert '/add/x/x' to the end of the URL. Where x is two numbers you would like to add.
  • For Subtraction you insert '/sub/x/x' to the end of the URL. Where x is two numbers you would like to subtract.
  • For Multiplication you insert '/multi/x/x' to the end of the URL. Where x is two numbers you would like to multiply.
  • For Division you insert '/div/x/x' to the end of the URL. Where x is two numbers you would like to divide.

For example:

  • 192.168.1.1:8080/add/2/4
  • 192.168.1.1:8080/sub/6/8
  • 192.168.1.1:8080/multi/2/4
  • 192.168.1.1:8080/div/2/4
